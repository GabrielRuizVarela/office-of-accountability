| Metric | Value | Period |

### Budget Collapse
- **2024 execution:** ARS $59,000M of $247,000M authorized = **24% execution**
- **Construction sector decline:** -29.5% (Jan-Sep 2024)
- **Firms destroyed:** ~4,000 construction companies
- **Jobs lost:** ~80,000 (est.)
- **Debt to constructors:** ~ARS $400,000M (Camarco claim)
- **2025 policy:** Only projects at 80%+ completion get funded

- **New model:** Red Federal de Concesiones — private operation and maintenance
- **Under tender:** 4,400km of strategic national routes

- **Pampa sector:** 546km (Route 5)
- **Companies bidding:** 15 firms competing for Buenos Aires/La Pampa corridors
- **Vialidad budget 2025:** >ARS $1 billion (+68% nominal), 75% for construction/maintenance
