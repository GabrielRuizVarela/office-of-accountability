## The Corporate Empire: SOCMA

**Sociedad Macri S.A. (SOCMA)** was founded by Franco Macri in January 1976. The group grew from 7 companies to 47 during the military dictatorship. At peak (Forbes 1998), Franco Macri's personal fortune was estimated at **USD 730 million**.

## The Correo Argentino Scandal

- **1997:** Menem privatized postal service, concession to SOCMA
- **1998-2001:** SOCMA paid canon only first year; debt reached **ARS 296M**
- **June 2016:** Macri's government accepted settlement - **98.82% reduction** of the debt (ARS 70B adjusted)
- **2017:** Prosecutor Boquin ruled it "equivalent to a condonation" and "abusive"
- **2017:** Prosecutor Zoni **charged President Macri** and Minister Aguad
- **2024:** Seven years later, family had still paid nothing

**This is the most direct documented conflict of interest: the President using state apparatus to settle his own family's debt to the state at a 99% discount.**

## Autopistas del Sol (AUSOL)

- AUSOL toll road concession renegotiated during Macri presidency
- Anti-Corruption Office recommended Macri not participate; he formally recused
- Despite recusal, 2018 renegotiation committed State to ~**USD 2 billion** in total economic impact
- **After toll increases authorized, Macri sold shares at 400% premium** to Natal Inversiones
- Prosecutors charged ex-officials with "fraudulent administration"

## Mariano Macri's 2024 Criminal Complaint

In August 2024, Mariano Macri filed criminal complaints against SOCMA naming Gianfranco, Florencia, and CEO Leonardo Maffioli. Charges: **fraudulent administration, document falsification, tax evasion, false balance sheets, and money laundering**. Centers on manipulation of Meinl Bank debt to benefit certain interests.
